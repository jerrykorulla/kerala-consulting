Business & Corporate Tours

Ideal for teams wanting to explore Kerala during business trips. Visit manufacturing hubs, tech parks, and local business centers. Network with local entrepreneurs and understand Kerala's business landscape.

Cultural & Heritage Tours

Discover Kerala's rich history and vibrant culture. Visit temples, palaces, churches, and museums. Experience traditional art forms, festivals, and witness the blend of cultures that defines Kerala.

Adventure Tours

For the adventurous spirit. Trekking, backwater kayaking, wildlife spotting, and water sports. Explore Kerala's natural beauty from Western Ghats to Arabian Sea.

Culinary Tours

Explore Kerala's famous cuisine. Visit spice markets, traditional cooking classes, local eateries, and fishing villages. Taste authentic Kerala dishes while learning about the region's gastronomic heritage.

Wellness & Ayurveda Tours

Rejuvenate with Kerala's traditional wellness practices. Ayurvedic treatments, yoga sessions, wellness retreats, and visits to traditional healing centers.

Sourcing & Trade Tours

For businesses looking to source products from Kerala. Visit production facilities, spice plantations, handcraft workshops, and connect with local suppliers and exporters.

Sample Itineraries

  • Day 1: Arrive in Kochi, city tour with historical old town, visit Fort Kochi and Chinese fishing nets
  • Day 2: Visit technology parks, local manufacturing units, meet with business community
